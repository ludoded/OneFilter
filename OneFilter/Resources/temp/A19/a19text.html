Hi,
I’m Jordan Price, creator of One Filter. I’m a UI/UX designer, graffiti artist, and father based in San Francisco, California.

I love taking photos with my daughter, especially in black & white. While there are many b&w photo filter apps out there, I found them to be either too bloated with extra features, or they they would take too long to fire up, and precious moments would be missed.

I created One Filter to be a fast, simple, and elegant solution to create beautiful black & white photographs on your iPhone. I want to help you capture beautiful moments forever.

Please feel free to reach out and say hello. I’d love to hear from you!

Happy memory creating!

-Jordan Price
